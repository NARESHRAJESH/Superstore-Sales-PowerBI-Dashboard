# 💰 Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI Desktop** to analyze revenue, profitability, sales trends, customer performance, and regional growth across different categories, ship modes, and customer segments.

The dashboard provides an executive-level view of retail sales performance through interactive KPI cards, DAX-driven calculations, trend analysis, and advanced drill-down visualizations.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to transform raw retail transaction data (Sample Superstore dataset) into meaningful business insights using Power BI.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand:

- Revenue and order performance
- Profitability trends over time
- Product category contribution
- Regional performance across the country
- Customer profitability and segmentation
- Top-performing customers

The project follows standard Business Intelligence practices including:

- Data cleaning using Power Query
- Star Schema data modeling
- Explicit DAX measure development
- Interactive dashboard design
- Business-focused data storytelling

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance using key KPIs.
- Analyze revenue and profit trends from 2014 to 2017.
- Identify top-performing regions and categories.
- Evaluate profit margin efficiency.
- Track monthly profit fluctuations.
- Identify high-value customers for targeted strategies.
- Perform root-cause analysis using interactive drill-downs.

---

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| 💰 Total Sales | Overall generated revenue |
| 📈 Total Profit | Total business profit |
| 📦 Total Orders | Total number of transactions |
| 👥 Total Customers | Unique customer count |
| 🎯 Profit Margin % | Profit efficiency percentage |

# 📈 Dashboard Features

## Executive KPI Overview

**Visual:**

- KPI Cards

**Displays:**

- Total Sales (2.30M)
- Total Profit (286.40K)
- Total Orders (5K)
- Total Customers (38K)
- Profit Margin (12.47%)

**Purpose:**

Provides a quick executive summary of overall retail sales and profitability performance.

---

## 📅 Sales Trend Over Time Analysis

**Visual:**

- Area Chart

**Purpose:**

- Analyze sales movement from 2014 to 2017.
- Identify growth patterns over the four-year period.
- Understand overall business trajectory.

---

## 🌍 Regional Sales Performance

**Visual:**

- Column Chart

**Purpose:**

- Compare sales performance across West, East, Central, and South regions.
- Identify top-performing markets.
- Understand geographical revenue contribution.

---

## 📦 Category & Ship Mode Profit Analysis

**Visuals:**

- Stacked Column Chart (Sales by Category and Region)
- Bar Chart (Profit Analysis by Ship Mode)

**Purpose:**

- Analyze revenue distribution across Technology, Office Supplies, and Furniture.
- Understand profit gains and losses based on shipping methods (First Class, Second Class, Standard Class).

---

## 👥 Customer Profitability & Value Matrix

**Visual:**

- Matrix Table

**Purpose:**

- Display top customers with their total Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Margin %.
- Identify high-value and high-margin customers.

---

## 🏆 Top 10 Customers by Sales

**Visual:**

- Horizontal Bar Chart

**Purpose:**

- Rank the highest-spending customers.
- Analyze their purchases segmented by Category (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture).

---

## 📉 Monthly Profit Trend

**Visual:**

- Line Chart

**Purpose:**

- Track monthly profit fluctuations across the tracked years.
- Identify seasonal dips and peaks in profitability.
- Evaluate business efficiency during specific months.

---

## 🌳 Sales Breakdown Root Cause Analysis

**Visual:**

- Power BI Decomposition Tree

**Purpose:**

Perform interactive drill-down analysis to understand factors affecting overall sales.

**Analysis Path:** Category ➔ Region ➔ Sub-Category

This helps answer:

- Which product category drives the most sales?
- Which region contributes most to the top category?
- Which sub-category is the strongest performer?

---

# 🎛 Interactive Filters

The dashboard includes slicers for:

- 🌍 Region (West, East, Central, South)
- 📦 Category (Technology, Office Supplies, Furniture)
- 🚚 Ship Mode (First Class, Second Class, Standard Class)
- 🏢 Segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

All visuals update dynamically based on user selections.
